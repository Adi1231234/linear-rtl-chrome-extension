# Privacy Policy

**Linear RTL** does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data.

The extension runs entirely on your device. It only reads text already present on `linear.app`
pages in order to set its display direction (right-to-left where appropriate). It makes no network
requests, uses no storage, and contains no analytics or tracking.

Because no data is handled, there is nothing to access, export, or delete.

Questions: open an issue at
<https://github.com/Adi1231234/linear-rtl-chrome-extension>.
